# CampusOne

> An AI-powered Smart College Assistant designed to simplify campus life by bringing academic resources, student services, and intelligent assistance into a single platform.

---

## Overview

CampusOne is a modern AI-powered college assistant that helps students manage their academic activities through a single, intuitive interface. Instead of navigating multiple portals for attendance, timetables, assignments, faculty information, and campus navigation, students can access everything from one application.

The project focuses on improving the overall student experience by combining AI-assisted interactions with a clean and user-friendly interface.

---

## Problem Statement

Students often rely on multiple applications and portals to access important academic information. This fragmented experience makes it difficult to quickly find schedules, assignments, faculty details, classroom locations, and other essential resources.

CampusOne addresses this challenge by providing a unified AI-powered platform that centralizes these services.
